Is this actually free? What's the catch?

No catch. The inspection is 100% free and there's zero obligation to move forward with any work. The roofing professional covers the cost. You get a full assessment of your roof's condition at no cost, period.

Will my insurance go up if I file a claim?

Storm damage claims are classified as "Acts of God" — they're different from at-fault claims. In most cases, premiums increase area-wide due to storm activity regardless of whether you personally file. Your matched pro can walk you through your specific policy.

How fast will someone contact me?

Most homeowners hear from their matched professional within a few hours during business hours. Inspections are typically scheduled within the same week.
