Thanks for Your Interest

Our free roof inspection service is currently available to homeowners only. Since the roof belongs to your landlord, they would need to authorize any inspection or repairs.

What you can do

Let your landlord know. If you've noticed any signs of roof damage — water stains on ceilings, missing shingles, or leaks during rain — let your landlord or property manager know. They can request a free inspection through our site and may be able to get repairs covered through their insurance at no cost.
